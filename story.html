Experience

Tilman's clients and collaborators range from big tech to scientific to artistic backgrounds.

Methods

Tilman use concepts of design thinking and agile development to assist story tellers across media and heritage to tell the essence of their stories. In workshops Tilman guides the participants with their seemingly limitless motivation and empathic persona. By gathering nuanced knowledge and experience about various creative processes across the globe, age groups and fields Tilman can help craft essentially rewarding experiences and products across media and platforms.
