I am a highly motivated and versatile Developer and UI/UX designer with more than 15+ years experience in the field of visualization, interaction design, user interfaces, and immersive experiences. I have worked as a consultant, developer, designer, and artist. My focus lies in bridging human needs and digital realities through responsible design, user experience, and art. As a developer I worked on single page applications and websites in React, Angular, Svelte, Django, SAP Hybris, Bloomreach, Symphony and JamStack services such as contentful, sanity, prismic. As a CTO  and project manager, I have led interdisciplinary teams of up to 15 people, working with boutique agencies, startups, and corporations.

I publicly discuss my experiences in tech through regular attendance at conferences and  in my research work as an artist, musician, and enthusiast media scientist, which allows me to maintain a nuanced perspective on a world in constant flux. Production, consumption, humanity, research, society, and aesthetics are a unified entity for me, and I am grateful for the opportunity to be involved in many exciting projects in these fields.
